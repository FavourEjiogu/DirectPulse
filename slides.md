
# DirectPulse: Healthcare at the Speed of Thought

---

## The Problem
*   **Overcrowded ERs:** 40% of ER visits are non-urgent.
*   **Misrouting:** Patients often see the wrong specialist first, wasting time.
*   **Logistics Gap:** Disconnect between diagnosis, pharmacy, and delivery.

---

## The Solution: DirectPulse
An end-to-end AI copilot that unifies triage, telemedicine, and logistics.

1.  **Analyze:** GenAI parses symptoms instantly.
2.  **Route:** Patients are sent to the specific specialist queue (not just a general pool).
3.  **Treat:** Doctors prescribe digitally.
4.  **Deliver:** Riders fulfill orders with secure verification.

---

## Key Technology: Gemini AI Integration
*   **Model:** Gemini 3.0 Flash Preview.
*   **Function:**
    *   Extracts medical entities.
    *   Determines triage severity (Red Flags).
    *   Suggests differential diagnoses.
    *   Provides "Human Error Checks" to reduce bias.

---

## User Journey
1.  **Patient:** Speaks symptoms -> AI Routes to Cardiologist.
2.  **Doctor:** Reviews AI summary -> Calls Patient -> Issues Rx.
3.  **Pharmacist:** Packs Rx -> Assigns Rider.
4.  **Rider:** Delivers -> Verifies OTP -> Complete.

---

## Security & Privacy
*   **Local Private Notes:** Encrypted client-side storage for personal health diaries.
*   **Secure Delivery:** OTP (One Time Password) required for physical handover of drugs.
*   **Role-Based Access:** Strict data separation between Rider (Logistics only) and Doctor (Clinical data).

---

## Future Roadmap
*   **Wearable Integration:** Real-time vitals sync (Apple Watch/Fitbit).
*   **Video Telemedicine:** WebRTC integration for video consults.
*   **Insurance API:** Real-time claims processing during payment.
